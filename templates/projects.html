Plant Disease Detection Web

Plant diseases are a major threat to food security, but their rapid identification remains difficult in many parts of the world due to the lack of the necessary infrastructure. The combination of increasing global smartphone penetration and recent advances in computer vision made possible by deep learning/Machine learning has paved the way for smartphone-assisted disease diagnosis. Using a public dataset of 1000 images of diseased and healthy plant leaves collected under controlled conditions, we train a deep convolutional neural network to identify 10 plant species and 12 diseases (or absence thereof). The trained model achieves an accuracy of greater 90% on a held-out test set, demonstrating the feasibility of this approach. Overall, the approach of training deep learning models on increasingly large and publicly available image datasets presents a clear path toward smartphone assisted plant disease diagnosis on a massive global scale.

Medicinal Plant Identification

Identification of Different Medicinal Plant through Image processing using machine learning algorithm. The main goal of our project is to detect the plant leaf and identify and their medicinal uses. Ayurveda is one of the ancient medicinal systems practiced in India for several thousands of years to treat various diseases with lower cost and undesirable side effects compared to allopathy medicine. Every organ of medicinal plants such as root, leaf, stem, fruit, and seed are composed of medicinal properties. The primary objective of creating an image dataset of Indian medicinal plant species is to promote the use of ayurvedic medicinal practices and spread the knowledge related to common medicinal plants that are present around us. The awareness would help in encouraging researchers, educators, and practitioners in the field of medicinal plants to address new challenges. Also, this would lead to an increased harvest of medicinal plants that leads to the adoption of the best health practices by common people and reducing health-related risks.

Railway OMS

Safeguarding the Rails: A Deep Dive into Railway Oscillation Monitoring Systems. Ensuring the safety and smooth operation of trains is paramount in the modern transportation landscape. Railway oscillation monitoring systems (OMS) play a crucial role in achieving this objective by vigilantly tracking and analyzing the oscillatory behavior of trains. These sophisticated systems act as the silent guardians of the rails, providing valuable insights that help prevent derailments, optimize maintenance, and enhance overall railway safety.
